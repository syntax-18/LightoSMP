The rules

Play fair. Play together.

Clear rules, fair gameplay, and quick bans for cheating or abuse.

⚔
Gameplay Rules
Keep survival fair for everyone.
  • No hacking, cheating, or use of unfair modifications.
  • No X-ray resource packs or hacked clients.
  • No exploiting bugs, glitches, or dupes.
  • Report exploits to staff immediately.
  • No griefing or intentionally ruining other players' builds.
  • Respect claimed land and private property.
💰
Economy Rules
Trade honestly and keep the economy balanced.
  • No scamming or misleading trades.
  • Do not abuse economy loopholes.
  • Real-world trading is prohibited unless approved by staff.
  • Do not create alternate accounts for unfair economic advantages.
🏠
Building Rules
Build with respect for the world and nearby players.
  • Keep builds appropriate and respectful.
  • Avoid building too close to other players without permission.
  • No lag machines or excessive redstone contraptions.
  • Do not create structures intended to disrupt gameplay.
🤖
